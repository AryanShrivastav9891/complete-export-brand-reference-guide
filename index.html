Keep ₹6,250 as an emergency buffer untouched in your account. One lost international shipment costs ₹2,500–₹4,000. One Etsy dispute could freeze a sale. Without a buffer, a single bad week ends the business. This is not optional.

Week-by-week execution plan
Week 1
Foundation
Research top 10 Etsy listings in your niche. Register "Kaasya" on Etsy, Instagram, and as .com domain. Design logo on Canva (Cormorant Garamond font). Set up Payoneer account.
Cost: ~₹800 (domain)
Week 2
First sourcing trip — Delhi
Go to Kinari Bazaar and Chawri Bazaar. Buy 2–3 samples of 8–10 products (₹1,500–2,000). Also buy packaging from Sadar Bazaar. Evaluate quality ruthlessly at home.
Cost: ₹3,000–4,000
Week 3
Photography and branding
Buy props (marble vinyl, dried flowers, linen fabric). Print stickers and thank you cards at local print shop. Spend one full day shooting 40–60 photos per product. Edit in Lightroom Mobile.
Cost: ₹1,500–2,000
Week 4
Etsy launch
Write all listing descriptions using the SEO formula. Set 13 Etsy tags per listing. List first 4–5 products. Do NOT tell friends yet — you want organic traffic data, not pity buys.
Cost: ₹250 (listing fees)
Week 5–6
Moradabad sourcing trip
Use your Etsy view data to know which products to buy more of. Train to Moradabad. Buy 10–15 pieces of top 3–4 products. This is your main inventory buy.
Cost: ₹7,000–8,000 (trip + inventory)
Week 7–8
Instagram and Pinterest launch
Post 3x/week on Instagram. Show sourcing trip footage, packing videos, styled flat lays. Create Pinterest boards: "Meditation Room Decor", "Brass Decor Inspiration". Pin all product photos.
Cost: ₹0
Week 9–10
First sales push
Start Etsy Ads at $5 USD/day max. DM 15–20 micro-influencers (5k–50k followers, yoga/wellness/home niche in USA/UK). Offer free product — no obligation to post. Goal: first 3–5 sales and reviews.
Cost: ₹3,500 (ads + 2–3 influencer shipments)
Week 11–12
Review and adjust
What sold? Double down on it. What didn't move? Fix the photos/description or remove it. Target by Week 12: 3–8 sales, 2–4 reviews. That is a successful launch — not a failure.
Cost: depends on reorder
Platform order — do not skip ahead
Etsy Start here — Month 1
Months 1–4 as your only platform
Built-in audience of buyers actively searching handmade and artisan items. Your target buyer shops here. Fees: $0.20 listing + 6.5% transaction + 3% payment processing.
Instagram + Pinterest Month 2
For traffic to Etsy, not direct selling yet
Reels showing sourcing trips, packing, artisan story. This authenticity is your biggest competitive edge — no Western seller can replicate it. Pinterest drives passive Etsy traffic for free.
Shopify Month 6+
Only after 20+ Etsy reviews and proven products
Costs $29 USD/month and you must drive all your own traffic. Starting before you have traction is wasted money. Use Etsy to validate first.
eBay — avoid entirely
Not suitable for premium brand positioning
eBay buyers hunt for discounts. Your premium pricing will be ignored. Your brand story is wasted. Do not list here.
